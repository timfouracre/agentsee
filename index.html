How it works

A cleaner alternative to agent leaderboards.

Instead of pushing “top agents”, AgentSee asks what matters to you and returns a report with matched agents and structured profile details.

1. Enter the basics

Start with postcode, property type, price band, bedrooms, and whether you are selling or letting.

2. Set your priorities

Choose service style, communication preferences, and the kind of marketing support you want.

3. Receive your report

View 3 to 5 matched agents with experience snapshots, service commitments, and direct contact options.

FAQ

Common questions.

Does AgentSee recommend estate agents?

No. It does not rank, recommend, or publicly score agents.

How are agents matched?

Using postcode coverage, property expertise, price-band experience, service commitments, and your priorities.

Can agents pay to appear more often?

No. Subscription revenue must not influence matching or visibility.

What happens after I receive a report?

You review the matched profiles and decide whether to contact an agent directly.
